Problem Context
- The exercice presents an interesting historical context through a story about three brothers trying to count their remaining soldiers using an ancient Chinese counting method, which leads to a mathematical problem involving modular arithmetic.

Technical Concepts Covered
- MiniZinc Programming

- Satisfaction problems vs. optimization problems
- Model construction without output statements
- Using the -a flag for all solutions
- Mathematical Concepts

- Modular arithmetic
- Chinese Remainder Theorem
- Simultaneous congruences
